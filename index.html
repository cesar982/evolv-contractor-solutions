Why The Money Disappears

It is not one big mistake. It is five small leaks on every job.

This happens to nearly every contractor in the trade. The work moves fast, the crew is focused on the job, and money slips between the field and the office. It is structural — not a reflection of how good your company is.

01

Undocumented change orders

Crew does the extra work. Nobody writes it up. The GC never gets billed for it.

02

Underbilled pay applications

You bill what you remember, not what you have actually earned. The gap adds up fast.

03

Retainage never chased

Money held on every job. At closeout the paperwork is not ready — so it never gets collected.

04

Submittal & closeout delays

Missing documents pause the work and push final payment further and further out.

05

The owner is the bottleneck

Everything administrative runs through one person. When that person is on a job, the office stops.
